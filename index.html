About Holberton School...

Holberton School is a project-based alternative education model that focuses on preparing individuals for careers in technology. The school was founded by Sylvain Kalache and Julien Barbier in 2015. Holberton School provides a hands-on and project-driven approach to learning software engineering, with an emphasis on practical skills and real-world projects.
